Description

The Archer is a ranged unit trained at the Archery Grounds. Armed with a bow and arrow, they attack from a distance, making them invaluable for raids and defenses. Archers are fragile but effective when positioned correctly away from melee combat.

Strategy

Archers are best used in combination with melee units. Position them behind your frontline to deal consistent ranged damage while melee units absorb damage. Their long range allows them to attack enemies before they close in. Upgrade to Composite Bow and Arrow in late game for increased damage output and reach.

Weapon Requirements

Archers require a Bow and Arrow for training. Bows and Arrows are crafted from 8 Wood and 40 Sticks, taking 30 seconds to complete. Ensure adequate stick production before training larger archer contingents, as sticks are resource-intensive to produce.
