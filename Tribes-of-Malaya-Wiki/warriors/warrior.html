Combat Overview

Combat Strategy: Effective armies combine melee units for frontline damage absorption with ranged units for consistent damage. Adjust your composition based on enemy defenses and available resources.

Melee Units are trained at the Training Grounds and excel at direct combat. They require various melee weapons (Spear, Sword, Stone Axe) and are suited for raids, defense, and sieges. Melee units typically have higher health but lower range compared to ranged units.

Ranged Units are trained at the Archery Grounds and attack from a distance. They require ranged weapons (Bow and Arrow, Gun) and are essential for dealing consistent damage while staying safe from melee attackers. Position them carefully to maximize their effectiveness.

Training Requirements

All warriors require food and the appropriate weapon for training. Make sure you have:

  • Adequate food production to sustain training
  • A Crafters Tent producing the required weapons
  • A Training Grounds or Archery Grounds depending on unit type
  • Sufficient storage capacity for resources

Plan your production chain carefully to avoid bottlenecks when scaling up your army.
