Description

The Axeman is a durable melee unit trained at the Training Grounds. Equipped with a stone axe, they have the highest health and armor of the basic melee units. Axemen are ideal for defensive operations and serving as frontline tanks in combat.

Strategy

Axemen excel at soaking damage and protecting other units. Their high armor and health make them perfect for defensive positions. Use them as your frontline while Swordsmen and Spearmen deal damage from behind. Due to their lower cost compared to other melee units, they're an economical choice for early defense.

Weapon Requirements

Axemen require a Stone Axe for training. Stone Axes are crafted from 2 Sticks and 1 Mineral, completing in 24 seconds. This makes them the most economical weapon to produce, allowing for rapid Axeman recruitment in early game.
