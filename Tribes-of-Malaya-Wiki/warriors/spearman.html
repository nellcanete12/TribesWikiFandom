Description

The Spearman is a fundamental melee unit trained at the Training Grounds. Equipped with a spear, these warriors form the backbone of any infantry force. They excel in direct combat and are cost-effective for early to mid-game military expansion.

Strategy

Spearmen are ideal for early raiding and defending your territory. Their relatively low training cost makes them accessible for rapid army building. They perform well in numbers and can hold their own against other melee units. Consider upgrading their weapons to Steel Spears for improved damage output in late-game encounters.

Weapon Requirements

Spearmen require a Spear for training. Spears are crafted from 20 Wood and 20 Mineral, taking 40 seconds to complete. Ensure you have adequate crafting capacity and mineral production before training large numbers of spearmen.
