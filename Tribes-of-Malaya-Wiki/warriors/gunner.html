Description

The Gunner is an advanced ranged unit trained at the Archery Grounds. Equipped with a gun, they deal significantly more damage than Archers and have superior range. Gunners are a mid-to-late game unit that provides powerful ranged firepower, though they require more resources to train.

Strategy

Gunners are perfect for heavy-hitting ranged attacks and can devastate enemies from a distance. Their higher damage and range make them ideal for sieges and coordinated attacks. Combine them with melee units for maximum effect. In late game, equip them with Steel Guns for overwhelming ranged dominance. Their higher food cost means they're best used when food production is abundant.

Weapon Requirements

Gunners require a Gun for training. Guns are crafted from 20 Wood and 8 Iron Nugget, yielding 2 guns in 32 seconds. Ensure you have consistent iron nugget production before training large numbers of gunners, as the mineral requirements are substantial.
