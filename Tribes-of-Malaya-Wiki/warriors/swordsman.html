Description

The Swordsman is a versatile melee unit trained at the Training Grounds. Armed with a sword, they deal higher damage than Spearmen but with slightly less armor. Swordsmen are excellent for aggressive play styles and closing out raids quickly with burst damage.

Strategy

Swordsmen excel in quick, aggressive raids due to their higher damage output. Mix them with Spearmen to create a balanced melee force. Their lower armor means they're best deployed with proper support or against lightly armored opponents. In late game, equip them with Steel Swords for devastating damage.

Weapon Requirements

Swordsmen require a Sword for training. Swords are crafted from 10 Wood and 10 Iron Nugget, yielding 2 swords in 32 seconds. Ensure you have iron nugget production available before training large contingents of swordsmen.
