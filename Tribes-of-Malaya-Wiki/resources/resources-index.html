Base Resources

The three foundation resources that fuel your entire economy. Every crafted item and settlement upgrade traces back to these raw materials.

Overview

Production Tick: All resources are produced every 5 minutes (300 seconds). Each assigned worker generates resources per tick based on their building type and level.

Tribes of Malaya has three fundamental base resources that are essential to your success. Your economy revolves around producing, managing, and utilizing these three resources efficiently. Understanding their production rates, sources, and uses is key to progressing through the game.

All Resources

Food
The lifeblood of your settlement. Building: Farmer's Tent | Max Workers: 72 | WM Rate: 0.25
Wood
Essential for construction and crafting. Building: Woodcutter's Tent | Max Workers: 30 | WM Rate: 0.3125
Minerals
Extracted from the earth by miners. Building: Miner's Tent | Max Workers: 30 | WM Rate: 0.3125

Production System

How Resources Are Produced
Each resource is produced by a specific production building. You can have multiple buildings of the same type to increase total production. Higher level buildings produce more resources per tick, and assigning more workers to a building increases its total output.
Worker Assignment
Each production building has a maximum number of workers it can support. Workers in Food production buildings have different rates than those in Wood or Mineral buildings. Plan your worker distribution carefully.
Storage & Overflow
Resources are produced every 5 minutes. If your storage is full, you'll lose the resources that tick — a permanent waste. Always maintain sufficient storage capacity for your production rate.

Strategic Progression

Early Game
Focus on establishing steady food and wood production. These two resources will support your initial growth and building construction.
Mid Game
Unlock mineral production by building the Miner's Tent. Minerals enable access to iron, steel, and advanced crafting recipes. This is a critical bottleneck in the game.
Late Game
Optimize production rates and worker distribution. Advanced progression requires massive amounts of all three resources. Efficient production chains become essential.

Key Tips

💡 Tip 1: Food production is the foundation of everything. Start with multiple Farmer's Tents and upgrade them steadily.
💡 Tip 2: Wood enters almost every recipe in the game. Establishing reliable wood production early prevents many bottlenecks.
💡 Tip 3: Minerals are a mid-game bottleneck. Don't overlook them once you unlock mining — they're critical for advancement.
💡 Tip 4: Never let your storage fill up — lost ticks are permanently lost production. Always build storage before you overflow.
