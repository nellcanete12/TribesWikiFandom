Description

Food is the lifeblood of your settlement. Every action in Tribes of Malaya requires food as a resource — from training warriors to crafting equipment. Without a steady food production, your city will grind to a halt. Food production is the foundation upon which all other activities are built.

How to Get Food

Build and upgrade the Farmer's Tent to produce food. Assign workers to increase production rate.

Food is produced by the Farmer's Tent building. To maximize food production:

1. Build multiple Farmer's Tents — More buildings = more total production
2. Upgrade them — Higher level buildings produce more food per tick
3. Assign workers — Up to 72 workers can be assigned to Farmer's Tents. Each worker produces at a rate of 0.25 food per tick
4. Calculate your needs — With 5-minute production ticks, plan your production based on your settlement's consumption

Usage

Food is used for virtually everything in Tribes of Malaya:

📋 Training Warriors — Every unit requires food to train
🏗️ Constructing Buildings — Settlement growth requires food investment
⚒️ Crafting Items — Most recipes require food as an ingredient
🏛️ Research & Upgrades — Improving your settlement consumes food
🛡️ Supporting Troops — Maintaining your army requires constant food supply

Strategic Tips

💡 Pro Tip: Food is the foundation of everything. Prioritize food production early in the game. A shortage of food will bottleneck all your other activities. Always maintain extra food storage to avoid production losses.
