Description

The Storage Tent stores food and other resources produced by your settlement. Your settlement has a limited storage capacity, and exceeding this capacity will prevent further resource production. Building and upgrading Storage Tents increases your storage capacity, allowing you to accumulate more resources. This building is essential for managing your settlement's resources effectively.

Storage Management

Each resource type has its own storage limit. Building more Storage Tents increases the capacity for all resource types. Monitor your storage levels regularly and build additional Storage Tents before reaching capacity. Running out of storage space can severely hamper your settlement's development.

Resource Accumulation

Storage Tents allow you to accumulate resources for future use. This is particularly important for expensive construction projects or military campaigns that require large amounts of resources. Plan your storage needs based on your settlement's goals and growth trajectory.

Strategy Tips

Build Storage Tents early and upgrade them regularly to maintain adequate storage capacity. Aim to have storage capacity that exceeds your production by a comfortable margin. This prevents resource production from being blocked and allows you to accumulate reserves for major projects. Consider the balance between storage and production buildings when planning your settlement layout.
