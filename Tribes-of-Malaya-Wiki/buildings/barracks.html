Description

The Barracks is a military building that houses soldiers and adds population capacity specifically for military units. Unlike Settler's Tents which house civilian population, the Barracks provides dedicated housing for trained warriors. Building Barracks is essential for maintaining a large military force, as they directly increase your soldier population capacity.

Population Management

Your settlement has a limited population capacity that is divided between settlers and soldiers. Settler's Tents increase settler capacity, while Barracks increase soldier capacity. To maintain a strong military, you must build sufficient Barracks to house all your trained units. Without adequate Barracks, trained soldiers cannot be stationed in your settlement.

Military Expansion

As you expand your military forces, ensure you have enough Barracks to accommodate all your soldiers. Building Barracks should be coordinated with your training facilities (Training Grounds and Archery Grounds) to maintain a balanced military expansion. Neglecting Barracks construction can limit your military potential.

Strategy Tips

Plan your military expansion carefully by building Barracks in advance of training new units. Consider the balance between settler and soldier population based on your strategic goals. A settlement focused on military conquest will need many Barracks, while a defensive settlement may need fewer.
