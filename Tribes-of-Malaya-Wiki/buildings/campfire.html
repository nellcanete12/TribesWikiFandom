Description

The Campfire is a social gathering point in your settlement. It serves as a central hub where your tribe gathers to build camaraderie and morale. While it doesn't directly produce resources, the Campfire is essential for maintaining the happiness and loyalty of your population. A well-maintained settlement with adequate gathering spaces ensures that your warriors remain motivated and ready for battle.

Strategic Importance

🏕️ Morale Booster — Increases settlement morale when built and upgraded
👥 Social Hub — Serves as a gathering point for your population
⚔️ Military Readiness — Higher morale leads to better warrior performance in combat
🎪 Cultural Center — Represents the heart of your tribe's identity

Construction & Placement

The Campfire is quick to build and requires minimal space in your settlement layout. It can be placed strategically in central locations for maximum visual impact and efficiency. Many players place it near other social buildings or at the heart of their settlement design.

💡 Placement Tip: Place your Campfire in a central, visible location. This not only improves aesthetics but also serves as a psychological rallying point for your tribe members.

Tips and Tricks

🎯 Placement Strategy
Position your Campfire at the center of your settlement where all paths converge. This creates a natural gathering point and improves the visual flow of your city layout.
📈 Upgrade Priority
While not a critical resource producer, upgrading your Campfire shows dedication to settlement quality of life. Higher levels provide better morale bonuses to surrounding buildings.
🏘️ Multiple Campfires
Building multiple Campfires can create distinct districts within your settlement, each serving as a local gathering point and improving neighborhood morale.

Gameplay Mechanics

Morale System
The Campfire contributes to your settlement's overall morale rating. Higher morale means your warriors are more effective in combat and your population is more productive.
Building Synergies
The Campfire works best when combined with other social buildings. Its effects are multiplicative when placed near taverns, markets, and other community spaces.
Upgrade Effects
Each upgrade level increases the morale radius and boost percentage. Level 10 Campfires provide significantly more benefit than level 1, making them worthwhile investments for long-term settlements.
