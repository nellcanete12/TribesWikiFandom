Description

The Scholar's Tent enables technology upgrades for your settlement. Technologies provide various benefits including improved production rates, enhanced military capabilities, and unlocked crafting recipes. This building is essential for long-term settlement development and can be upgraded up to level 5. Higher-level Scholar's Tents may unlock more advanced technologies or reduce technology research times.

Technology System

Technologies are permanent improvements that enhance your settlement's capabilities. Once researched, technologies provide lasting benefits to your settlement. Different technologies have different prerequisites and requirements. Building Scholar's Tents allows you to research technologies that unlock new possibilities and improve existing operations.

Long-Term Development

Investing in technology research through Scholar's Tents is crucial for long-term settlement success. Early technologies provide foundational improvements, while advanced technologies unlock powerful capabilities. Plan your technology research path based on your settlement's strategic goals.

Strategy Tips

Build a Scholar's Tent early in your settlement's development to begin researching technologies. Prioritize technologies that align with your settlement's strategic focus. Upgrade your Scholar's Tent to unlock more advanced technologies. Consider the technology tree carefully to maximize the benefits of your research investments.
