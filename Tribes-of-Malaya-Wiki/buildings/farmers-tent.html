Description

The Farmer's Tent enables the gathering of food on fertile land. Food is the most essential resource in Tribes of Malaya, as it is required for nearly every action including construction, training, and crafting. Place this building on fertile land to maximize food production. The Farmer's Tent can be upgraded up to level 10, significantly increasing its production capacity.

Placement Requirements

This building must be placed on fertile land tiles to function. Fertile land is typically identified by its distinctive appearance on the map. Building on non-fertile land will not produce food.

Upgrade Benefits

Each upgrade level increases the food production rate. With 10 possible upgrade levels, the Farmer's Tent is one of the most upgradeable production buildings in the game. Prioritize upgrading these buildings to ensure a steady food supply for your growing settlement.

Strategy Tips

Build multiple Farmer's Tents on all available fertile land to maximize food production. Food is crucial for all settlement activities, so maintaining a strong food economy is essential. Consider the placement and upgrade priorities of your Farmer's Tents as part of your overall resource strategy.
