Description

The Woodcutter's Tent enables the gathering of wood on forest land. Wood is a critical resource used in construction and crafting. Place this building on forest terrain to harvest wood efficiently. With a maximum of 5 upgrade levels, the Woodcutter's Tent can significantly increase wood production to support your settlement's growth.

Placement Requirements

This building must be placed on forest land tiles to function. Forest land is easily recognizable on the map. Building on non-forest terrain will not produce wood.

Resource Uses

Wood is essential for many construction projects and crafting recipes. Maintaining a steady supply of wood is important for expanding your settlement and creating items. Consider your wood needs when planning your production buildings.

Strategy Tips

Build Woodcutter's Tents on all available forest land to maximize wood production. While wood has fewer upgrade levels than food production, it remains an important resource. Balance your wood production with your settlement's construction and crafting needs.
