Description

The Settler's Tent is a basic shelter designed to house a single settler family. It serves as the foundation of any settlement, providing essential housing for your population. As your settlement grows, you can upgrade this building to accommodate more settlers and improve living conditions.

Usage

Settler's Tents are essential for maintaining your population. Each upgraded tent can house more settlers, increasing your workforce capacity. This directly impacts your ability to gather resources and expand your settlement. Prioritize upgrading these buildings early to ensure steady population growth.

Strategy Tips

Build multiple Settler's Tents to increase your population capacity. Higher-level tents provide better accommodation and can house more families. Balance your construction between housing and production buildings to maintain a healthy settlement economy.
