Description

The Watch Tower is a tall defensive structure that provides a significant defensive advantage to your settlement. This building requires substantial resources to construct, reflecting its strategic importance. Watch Towers are particularly valuable for defending against enemy attacks and can be strategically placed around your settlement's perimeter to maximize defensive coverage.

Defensive Benefits

Watch Towers provide elevated positions for ranged units to defend your settlement. They offer superior vantage points for spotting and engaging enemies from a distance. The presence of Watch Towers can significantly improve your settlement's defensive capabilities and deter enemy attacks.

Strategic Placement

Place Watch Towers strategically around your settlement's perimeter to create overlapping defensive zones. Consider the terrain and potential attack vectors when planning Watch Tower placement. Multiple Watch Towers working together can create a formidable defensive network.

Strategy Tips

Build Watch Towers once your settlement is established and you have secured sufficient resources. They are most valuable for settlements in contested areas or those under threat of attack. Consider your settlement's location and potential enemies when deciding how many Watch Towers to construct. A well-defended settlement with multiple Watch Towers can withstand significant military pressure.
