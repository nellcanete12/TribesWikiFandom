Description

The Market enables trading of goods with other settlements and players. Trading is a crucial economic activity that allows you to exchange surplus resources for needed items. The Market can be upgraded up to level 2, with higher levels potentially improving trading rates or unlocking additional trading options. A functional Market is essential for a thriving settlement economy.

Trading System

The Market facilitates commerce between settlements. You can trade surplus resources for items you need, creating economic interdependence between settlements. Trading can be advantageous for both parties, allowing settlements to specialize in certain resources while obtaining others through trade.

Economic Strategy

A well-developed Market enables sophisticated economic strategies. You can focus your settlement's production on specific resources and trade for others, potentially achieving better overall efficiency. Trading can also be used diplomatically to build relationships with other settlements.

Strategy Tips

Build a Market once your settlement has established production and has surplus resources to trade. Identify which resources your settlement produces in abundance and which it lacks. Establish trading relationships with other settlements to create mutually beneficial exchanges. Upgrade your Market to unlock better trading opportunities. A strong trading network can significantly enhance your settlement's economic prosperity.
