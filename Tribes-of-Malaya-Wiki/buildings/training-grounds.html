Description

The Training Grounds is a military building that enables the training of melee units. Melee warriors are essential for ground combat and defense. This building requires significant resources to construct, reflecting its importance to your military capabilities. Train melee units here to build a strong ground force for both offensive and defensive operations.

Unit Training

Melee units trained at the Training Grounds are effective in close-range combat. They form the backbone of ground-based military operations. Different tribes may have access to different melee unit types with varying stats and abilities. Consult the Warriors section of the wiki for detailed information about available melee units.

Military Strategy

Building a balanced military force is crucial for success in Tribes of Malaya. The Training Grounds should be paired with the Archery Grounds to create a diverse military composition. Melee units excel in direct combat but are vulnerable to ranged attacks, so consider your overall military strategy when allocating resources.

Strategy Tips

Construct Training Grounds once you have secured sufficient resources and established a stable economy. Maintain a steady supply of melee units to defend your settlement and support offensive operations. Balance your military spending with resource production to ensure long-term sustainability.
