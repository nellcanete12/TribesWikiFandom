Description

The Miner's Tent enables the gathering of minerals on mineral land. Minerals are valuable resources used in advanced crafting and construction. Place this building on mineral-rich terrain to extract minerals efficiently. With a maximum of 5 upgrade levels, the Miner's Tent can substantially increase mineral production.

Placement Requirements

This building must be placed on mineral land tiles to function. Mineral land is typically found in rocky or mountainous areas on the map. Building on non-mineral terrain will not produce minerals.

Resource Importance

Minerals are essential for advanced crafting recipes and high-level construction projects. They are often the most limited resource in early-game settlements, making mineral production a strategic priority. Secure access to mineral land early and prioritize upgrading your Miner's Tents.

Strategy Tips

Identify and secure mineral-rich areas on your map early in the game. Build Miner's Tents on all available mineral land and upgrade them to maximize mineral production. Minerals become increasingly important as your settlement advances, so maintaining a strong mineral economy is crucial for long-term success.
