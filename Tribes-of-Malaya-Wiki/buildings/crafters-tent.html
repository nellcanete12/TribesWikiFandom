Description

The Crafter's Tent enables the crafting of items using raw materials and resources. Crafting is essential for creating advanced items, weapons, armor, and other valuable goods. This building can be upgraded up to level 8, with higher levels potentially reducing craft times or increasing crafting efficiency. The Crafter's Tent is fundamental to your settlement's economic development.

Crafting System

The Crafter's Tent allows you to transform raw resources into finished products. Different crafting recipes require different combinations of ingredients and produce various items. Refer to the Crafting section of the wiki for a complete list of available recipes and their requirements. Building multiple Crafter's Tents allows you to run multiple crafting operations simultaneously.

Economic Importance

Crafting is a key component of your settlement's economy. Crafted items can be used for various purposes including equipment for your military, trade goods for commerce, or resources for advanced construction. A well-developed crafting infrastructure can significantly boost your settlement's prosperity.

Strategy Tips

Build Crafter's Tents early to begin crafting valuable items. Upgrade them to improve efficiency and reduce crafting times. Build multiple Crafter's Tents to run parallel crafting operations and maximize production. Focus on crafting items that are in high demand or that support your settlement's strategic goals.
