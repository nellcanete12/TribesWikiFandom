Description

The Archery Grounds is a military building that enables the training of ranged units. Ranged warriors are effective at attacking from a distance, providing tactical advantages in combat. This building requires the same resources as the Training Grounds, making it equally important to your military infrastructure. Train ranged units here to add versatility to your military force.

Unit Training

Ranged units trained at the Archery Grounds excel at dealing damage from a distance. They are particularly effective against melee units and can provide support fire during battles. Different tribes may have access to different ranged unit types with varying stats and abilities. Refer to the Warriors section for comprehensive unit information.

Military Composition

A balanced military force typically includes both melee and ranged units. Ranged units provide tactical flexibility and can engage enemies before they reach your melee forces. The combination of Training Grounds and Archery Grounds allows you to create a well-rounded military capable of handling various combat scenarios.

Strategy Tips

Build Archery Grounds alongside Training Grounds to create a balanced military force. Ranged units are particularly valuable for defending against enemy attacks and for sieging enemy settlements. Maintain a healthy ratio of ranged to melee units based on your tactical needs and available resources.
