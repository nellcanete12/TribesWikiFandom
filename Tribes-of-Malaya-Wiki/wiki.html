Early Game Build Order

Strategic Priority: Food is the foundation of everything. Start with production and storage before moving into military expansion.
1
Farmer's Tent
Food is the foundation of everything. Start here — every other action requires food. Build multiple and upgrade them steadily to level 10.
2
Woodcutter's Tent
Wood enters almost every recipe in the game. Get this running early so you never bottleneck on lumber for crafting or building.
3
Storage Tent
Prevent resource waste. Build storage before you overflow — lost ticks are permanently lost production.
4
Crafter's Tent
Unlock crafting immediately. Without it, raw resources sit idle with no progression path. Essential for all weapon and material production.
5
Miner's Tent
Opens up Iron Nuggets and Steel. Also enables Gold Dust for Taga-Ilog. Minerals are a mid-game bottleneck without this building.

Base Resources

Production Tick: Resources are produced every 5 minutes (300 seconds). Each assigned worker generates resources per tick based on their building type.
Food
The lifeblood of your settlement. Building: Farm | Max Workers: 72 | WM Rate: 0.25
Wood
Essential for construction and crafting. Building: Woodcutter | Max Workers: 30 | WM Rate: 0.3125
Minerals
Extracted from the earth by miners. Building: Mine | Max Workers: 30 | WM Rate: 0.3125
